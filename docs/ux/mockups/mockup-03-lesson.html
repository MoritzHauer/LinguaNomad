Hello (formal)
Greeting · Formal register

In context

— Саламатсызбы, мугалим? — Salamatsızby, mugalim? — Hello, teacher?
— Саламатсыз, Айгүл! — Salamatsız, Aygül! — Hello, Aigul!
💡 Grammar note: Formal vs. informal
▾
Саламатсызбы uses the formal -сыз suffix, which marks respect. Use it with elders, teachers, and strangers. For friends and peers, use Саламбы instead. Vowel harmony affects the suffix endings throughout Kyrgyz.
