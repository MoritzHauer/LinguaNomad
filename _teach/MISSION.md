# Mission: Kyrgyz

## Why
Learn Kyrgyz to make travel less opaque and to begin speaking with family members in a way that feels respectful and practical. The first milestone is to stop seeing written Kyrgyz as unreadable noise and start decoding signs, names, and simple words.

## Success looks like
- Read Kyrgyz Cyrillic slowly enough to sound out common signs, names, and short messages.
- Recognize and pronounce the Kyrgyz-specific letters without freezing.
- Build toward basic family and travel conversations from a script-first foundation.

## Constraints
- Starting from zero.
- Does not yet know the Kyrgyz alphabet.
- Early lessons should be short, practical, and directly usable.

## Out of scope
- Historical Kyrgyz scripts beyond what is needed for orientation.
- Deep grammar before basic reading fluency exists.
- Russian as a prerequisite.
