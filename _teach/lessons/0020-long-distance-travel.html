Grammar: Dative Destination — "To Bishkek"

To say "to [place]", add the dative suffix to the destination name.

  • After a voiceless consonant → -КА / -КЕ
  • After a voiced consonant or vowel → -ГА / -ГЕ
  • БИШКЕК + КЕ = БИШКЕККЕ — to Bishkek
  • ОШ + КО = ОШКО — to Osh
  • ВОКЗАЛ + ГА = ВОКЗАЛГА — to the train station

You have used this dative pattern in lessons 0015 (МАГА), 0017 (ОҢГО), and 0019 (ЖАККА). It is one of the most productive suffixes in Kyrgyz.
