Lesson 0033

Verb Tenses — Past, Present, Future

Form and recognise the three tense families in everyday Kyrgyz sentences. One root verb, three patterns — and you can place any action in time.

Mission: grammar Skill: conversation Time: 15–20 minutes

Builds on: Lesson 0032 — The Six Cases

Present Progressive

Use this tense for actions happening right now.

Pattern: stem + -ып/-ип/-уп/-үп + ЖАТ + personal suffix

PersonFormEnglish
Мен (I)барып жатамI am going (right now)
Сиз (you, formal)барып жатасызYou are going
Ал (he/she)барып жататHe/she is going

ЖАТ is the auxiliary verb "to lie/be in progress". It carries the tense; the main verb converb ends in -ып/-ип.

Simple Present / Habitual

Use this tense for habitual, repeated, or general actions — and also for the future when a time word makes it clear.

Pattern: stem + -А/-Е/-ЙТ + personal suffix

PersonFormEnglish
МенбарамI go (habitually) / I'll go
СизбарасызYou go / You'll go
АлбаратHe/she goes / will go

Past Tense

Use this tense for completed actions in the past.

Pattern: stem + -ДЫ/-ДИ/-ТЫ/-ТИ + personal suffix

PersonFormEnglish
МенбардымI went
СизбардыңызYou went (formal)
АлбардыHe/she went

Vowel harmony applies: БАР- (back vowel root) takes -ДЫ; ЖЕ- (front vowel root) takes -ДИ → жедим (I ate).

Personal Suffix Reference

Person Habitual / Future Past Progressive
Мен (I)-м-ым/-им-м
Сиз (you, formal)-сыз/-сиз-ыңыз/-иңиз-сыз/-сиз
Ал (he/she)-т/-ат/-ет(no suffix)-т

БАР- conjugated across tenses

  • Мен барам. — I go / I'll go.
  • Мен барып жатам. — I am going (now).
  • Мен бардым. — I went.

ЖЕ- (to eat) alongside

  • Мен жейм. — I eat.
  • Мен жеп жатам. — I am eating (now).
  • Мен жедим. — I ate.
