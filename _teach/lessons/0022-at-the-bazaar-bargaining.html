Lesson 0022

At the Bazaar — Bargaining

Bargaining is expected at Kyrgyz bazaars. Learn the phrases that open a negotiation, push back on a price, and close a deal — without embarrassing yourself or walking away empty-handed.

Mission: travel Skill: shopping Time: 10–15 minutes

Builds on: Lesson 0021 — At the Kiosk

Opening a Bid

When you see something you want, start by asking the price — then react:

Канчага? — How much? Кымбат! — Expensive!

After declaring Кымбат!, pause. The seller will often drop the price without further prompting. Follow up with your counter-offer:

… сомго берет? — Will you sell for … som?

Use a specific number to anchor the negotiation. Sellers expect it.

Counter-Offer and Closing

If the seller counters, you can keep pushing or accept:

  • Баа чейин. — Let's bring the price down.
  • Акыркы баа? — Is that your final price?
  • Ошондо алам. — Then I'll take it.

If the item isn't right, try: Бул жакшы эмес (this isn't good quality) or Башкасы барбы? (do you have another one?).
