Lesson 0012

Numbers 21–100 & Ordinals

Form any two-digit number and say first, second, third — for floors, bus stops, and apartment numbers.

Ordinals: First, Second, Third…

Add the suffix -нчы / -нчи (vowel-harmony matched) to a cardinal number to form the ordinal. The vowel inserted before the suffix matches the vowel harmony of the stem.

Cardinal Ordinal English
бир (1)биринчиfirst
эки (2)экинчиsecond
үч (3)үчүнчүthird
төрт (4)төртүнчүfourth
беш (5)бешинчиfifth
он (10)онунчуtenth

The suffix varies because Kyrgyz vowel harmony requires back vowels (ы/у) with back-vowel stems and front vowels (и/ү) with front-vowel stems. For everyday use, биринчи through бешинчи cover almost everything you need.

Real-World Uses: Floors, Stops, Apartments

Биринчи кабат — first floor. You will see this on lift buttons and building directories across Bishkek.

Экинчи аялдама — second stop. When a driver or passenger tells you how many stops to go, ordinals are the form used.

Квартира жыйырма үч — apartment 23. Apartment numbers use cardinals, not ordinals — so this knowledge and lesson 0011 together give you full coverage.
