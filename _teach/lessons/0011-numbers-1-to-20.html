Pattern Alert: 11–19 Are Just ОН + Digit

Он (10) + any digit from 1–9 gives you 11–19 automatically. No irregular forms, no changes. Он беш = 10 + 5 = 15. Он тогуз = 10 + 9 = 19. The only number that breaks the pattern is 20: жыйырма must be memorised on its own.

Numbers In the Wild

Here is where you will actually hear these numbers in Bishkek or any Kyrgyz-speaking town:

At the kiosk

A vendor calls out the price: Он беш сом — fifteen som. Numbers go before the currency unit сом.

Marshrutka route

The route number is stated directly: Маршрутка жети — marshrutka number seven. No ordinal, just the cardinal.

Apartment number

A friend says their flat number: Квартира он эки — apartment twelve. Same pattern: number follows the noun it belongs to.

Bus stop

You hear the stop announced: Аялдама тогуз — stop nine. When in doubt, the number by itself is usually enough to get understood.
