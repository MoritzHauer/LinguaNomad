Older vs Younger: There Is No Single Word for "Sibling"

English has one word for sibling; Kyrgyz has four — all dependent on the age relationship. ага is only an older brother; эже is only an older sister. The younger equivalents are ини (younger brother) and карындаш (younger sister). Getting this right matters immediately when you introduce your family, because using the wrong word implies the wrong age relationship.

The suffixes -сы / -си and -ы / -и on иниси and карындашы signal possession. For now, use the bare forms ини / карындаш when in doubt — possessive suffixes are covered in lesson 0034.

Six Core Terms

These six words carry most of the weight in everyday family conversation. Drill them until you can produce each one without hesitation.

АТА

ata · father

АПА

apa · mum

АГА

aga · older brother

ЭЖЕ

ezhe · older sister

УУЛ

uul · son

КЫЗ

kyz · girl / daughter

чоң = big / great

Grandparent words are formed by placing чоң (big / great) before the parent word. The pattern is transparent and easy to remember.
