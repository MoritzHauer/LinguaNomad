Lesson 0010

Talking About Your Family

Say who you have, who you don't have, and how many — in three or four Kyrgyz sentences.

Mission: family Mission: travel Time: 15 minutes

Builds on: Lesson 0009 — Family Vocabulary · Lesson 0007 — Introducing Yourself

The БАР / ЖОК Pattern

БАР means "exists / is present" — used where English says "I have". ЖОК is the negative: "doesn't exist / not present." The pattern is МЕН + [family member] + БАР/ЖОК.

Kyrgyz English
Менин апам барI have a mum (literally: my mum exists)
Менин агам жокI don't have an older brother
Менде бир ини барI have one younger brother (менде = for me)
Менин эки агам барI have two older brothers
Менин кызым жокI don't have a daughter

Possessive Suffix: -М (my)

Adding -М to a family word makes it "my [family member]". After a vowel, attach -м directly. After a consonant, insert a vowel: -ым (back vowels) or -им (front vowels).

Base + suffix Meaning
атаатамmy father
апаапамmy mum
агаагамmy older brother
кызкызымmy daughter

Building a Family Description

Follow these three steps to assemble three or four sentences about your own family:

  1. State whether you have a father: Менин атам бар. or Менин атам жок.
  2. State whether you have a mum: Менин апам бар. or Менин апам жок.
  3. Add counts for siblings or children: Менде [number] [family member] бар.

Template:

Менин атам [бар/жок]. Менин апам [бар/жок]. Менде [number] [family member] бар.

Example: Менин атам бар. Менин апам бар. Менде эки ага бар. Менин кызым жок. — I have a father. I have a mum. I have two older brothers. I don't have a daughter.

Positive Sentences

  • Менин атам бар — I have a father
  • Менин апам бар — I have a mum
  • Менде эки ага бар — I have two older brothers

Negative Sentences

  • Менин агам жок — I don't have an older brother
  • Менин кызым жок — I don't have a daughter
  • Менде иним жок — I don't have a younger brother

Asking About Family: -БЫ/-БИ Questions

Add -БЫ (after back vowels) or -БИ (after front vowels) to the end of a statement to turn it into a yes/no question. No word order change needed.

Kyrgyz Transliteration English
Сиздин балаңыз барбы?Sizdin balanyz barby?Do you have children?
Сиздин апаңыз барбы?Sizdin apanyz barby?Do you have a mum (alive)?

Сиздин = your (formal). The -ңыз ending is the formal possessive. Use this form with elders or people you have just met.
