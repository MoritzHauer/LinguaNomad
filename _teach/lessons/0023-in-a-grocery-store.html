In a Grocery Store

Ask for a kilo of potatoes, two pieces of fruit, half a kilo of garlic, and find out the total — a complete basic grocery shop in Kyrgyz.

Asking for a Quantity

Combine a number or unit with the item name to make a request:

Бир кило картошка Эки даана алма Жарым кило пияз

Add бар? to ask availability, or just say the phrase as a request and point. Кiosk-style brevity is perfectly natural.

ДААНА is used for individual pieces of countable things: fruit, eggs, bread rolls. Use КИЛОГРАММ for bulk goods sold by weight.

Totalling Up

When you are done selecting, ask for the total with:

Баары канча? — How much is everything?

Before leaving, ask for a bag if you need one:

Пакет барбы? — Do you have a bag?

Staff may quote per-kilo prices spontaneously: Жарым кило беш сом — half a kilo is five som. Listen for the unit + price pattern.
