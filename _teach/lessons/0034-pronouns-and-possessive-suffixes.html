Lesson 0034

Pronouns & Possessive Suffixes

Learn all seven personal pronouns and attach the right possessive suffix to any family-member word. These patterns also appear in verb endings — so learning them pays double.

Mission: family Skill: grammar Time: 15–20 minutes

Builds on: Lesson 0033 — Verb Tenses · Lesson 0030 — Agglutination

АГА (older brother) follows the same back-vowel pattern: агам, агаң, агаңыз, агасы, агабыз.

You Already Know These Patterns

You have already used possessive suffixes in earlier lessons without naming them:

  • Менин апам бар (lesson 0010) — I have a mother. апам = apa + -м (my).
  • Сиздин атыңыз ким? (lesson 0007) — What is your father's name? атыңыз = ata + -ңыз (your, formal).

This lesson names the pattern so you can apply it to any noun, not just the ones you have already seen.
