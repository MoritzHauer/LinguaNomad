Lesson 0024

Clothes & Department Store

Ask for your size, say which colour you want, and request to try something on — everything you need to shop for clothing in Kyrgyz.

Mission: travel Skill: shopping Time: 10–15 minutes

Asking About Size

State your size first, then ask for the item:

Менин өлчөмүм 42. Чоңураак барбы? Кичинеирек барбы?

Both өлчөм and the Russian loanword размер are understood in shops. Use whichever comes naturally.

To ask to try something on: Кийип көрсөм болобу?

Asking About Colour

Name the colour and add … түсүндө барбы?:

Кызыл түсүндө барбы? Көк барбы?

You can shorten the phrase to just [colour] барбы? — shop assistants will understand.

КӨК covers both blue and dark blue in everyday usage. For navy specifically, you may hear кара-көк (dark blue, lit. black-blue).
