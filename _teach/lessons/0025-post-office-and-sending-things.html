Post Office & Sending Things

Post a letter or parcel, buy a stamp, and fill in the basic fields on a sending form — all in Kyrgyz.

Filling in a Form

At the post office you will be asked to fill in a standard form. These are the three fields you need to recognise:

Field label Transliteration What to write
Аты-жөнүAty-zhönüFull name of sender / recipient
ДарекDarekStreet address
ИндексIndeksPostcode

Staff at the post office will often point to the fields and say Жазыңыз (please write) — you will know exactly what they mean after this lesson.
