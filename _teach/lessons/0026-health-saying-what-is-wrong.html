Lesson 0026

Health — Saying What Is Wrong

Tell a doctor or pharmacist what hurts, understand basic advice, and know how to call for urgent help. These phrases matter more than almost anything else you will learn.

The ООРУЙТ Pattern

In Kyrgyz you say "[body part]–М ООРУЙТ" to mean "my [body part] hurts". The suffix -М (or -ЫМ / -ИМ) marks the first-person possessive — "my".

баш + -ым → башым ооруйт ич + -им → ичим ооруйт бел + -им → белим ооруйт

Learn the pattern once and you can report any pain. Find the body-part word, add the possessive suffix, then say ооруйт.
