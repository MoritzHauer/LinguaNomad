Lesson 0019

Taxis & Negotiating the Fare

Hail a taxi, state where you're going, and agree on a price before you sit down — haggling is expected.

Mission: travel Skill: taxis & bargaining Time: 15 minutes

Builds on: Lesson 0015 — Ordering at a Café; Lesson 0018 — City Transport

Hailing and Stating Your Destination

Kyrgyz Transliteration English
Такси!Taksi!Taxi! (flagging down)
… жакка… zhakkaTo … (dative — direction)
Мени … жакка жеткирMeni … zhakka zhetkirTake me to … (imperative)
БорборгоBorborgoTo the centre
АэропорткоAeropartkoTo the airport

Grammar note: ЖАККА = direction suffix. МЕНИ = me (accusative of МЕН). ЖЕТКИР = deliver / take (imperative).

Negotiating the Fare

Always agree on the price before you get in. The template … СОМГО БОЛОБУ? is your counter-offer.

Kyrgyz Transliteration English
Канча турат?Kancha turat?How much does it cost?
… сом… som… som (driver's price)
Кымбат!Kymbat!Expensive! / That's too much!
… сомго болобу?… somgo bolob?Will you do it for … som?
БолотBolotOK / That works
БолбойтBolboytNo / That doesn't work
Арзан!Arzan!Cheap! (acknowledging a good deal)

Common Destinations in Bishkek

Ала-Тоо аянты — Ala-Too Square Ош базары — Osh Bazaar Борбор — city centre Аэропорт — airport

Add -КА/-КЕ/-ГА/-ГЕ/-КО/-ГО (dative) to get "to" the destination: Ош базарыга, Борборго, Аэропортко.
