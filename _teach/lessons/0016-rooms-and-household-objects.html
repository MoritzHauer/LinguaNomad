The Locative Case: "In / At / On"

Add the suffix -ДА/-ДЕ (or -ТА/-ТЕ) to a noun to say "in", "at", or "on" it. This is the locative case.

  • After a voiced consonant or vowel → -ДА / -ДЕ (vowel harmony applies)
  • After a voiceless consonant → -ТА / -ТЕ
  • When the noun ends in a vowel, the connector -Н- is added before the suffix

Where Is It? — Five Example Sentences

In Kyrgyz, word order is flexible but verb-final. Subject + location works well for simple statements.

Kyrgyz Transliteration English
Стол бөлмөдө.Stol bölmödö.The table is in the room.
Терезе ашкананда.Tereze ashkanananda.The window is in the kitchen.
Кровать жатак бөлмөдө.Krovat zhatak bölmödö.The bed is in the bedroom.
Чырак үйдө.Chyrak üydö.The lamp is in the house.
Менин бөлмөм үйдө.Menin bölmöm üydö.My room is in the house.

Note: АШКАНА ends in a vowel, so the suffix chain is АШКАНА + Н + ДА = АШКАНАНДА.
