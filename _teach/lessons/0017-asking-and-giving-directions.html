Asking & Giving Directions

Grammar: Dative -ГА/-КА = "to / toward"

The direction words ОҢГО and СОЛГО already carry the dative suffix -ГО/-КО. This suffix marks motion toward a destination.

  • After a voiced consonant or vowel → -ГА / -ГЕ / -ГО (vowel harmony)
  • After a voiceless consonant → -КА / -КЕ / -КО
  • ОНГ + ГО = ОҢГО — to the right
  • СОЛ + ГО = СОЛГО — to the left

You met the dative case in lesson 0015 (МАГА = to me). Here it appears on direction words and destinations.
