Asking About the Weather

The standard opener is:

Аба ырайы кандай? — What's the weather like?

АБА = air / atmosphere, ЫРАЙЫ = condition/state, КАНДАЙ = what kind / how is it?

You can also just ask: Жаап жатабы? — Is it raining?

Answering About the Weather

Short answers work perfectly:

Жылуу — warm Суук — cold Булуттуу — cloudy Жаап жатат — raining

For a forecast, add a time word: Эртең жылуу болот — tomorrow it will be warm.

БОЛОТ is the future form of the verb "to be". You will study verb tenses in detail in lesson 0033.
