Lesson 0028

Daily Routine

Describe a typical day in five or six Kyrgyz sentences — the verbs, the time words, and a sample description you can adapt to your own schedule.

Mission: family Skill: conversation Time: 10–15 minutes

Builds on: Lesson 0027 — Weather Expressions

Daily Routine Verbs (1st person)

Kyrgyz Transliteration English
турамturamI get up
ичемichemI drink
жуунамzhuunamI wash (myself)
жеймzheymI eat
барамbaramI go
кайтамkaytamI return
уктаймuktaymI sleep
иштеймishteymI work
окуймokuyumI read / I study
коёмkoyomI put / I place

Time Expressions

Kyrgyz Transliteration English
эртең мененerteng menenin the morning
күндүзүkündüzüduring the day
кечиндеkechindein the evening
түндеtündeat night
саат алтыдаsaat altidaat six o'clock

The pattern for clock times is: саат + number + -да/-де/-та/-те (locative case). Саат жетиде = at seven o'clock.

A Sample Daily Description

Kyrgyz Transliteration English
Мен саат жетиде турам. Men saat zhetide turam. I get up at seven.
Мен эртең менен чай ичем. Men erteng menen chay ichem. I drink tea in the morning.
Саат сегизде иштейм. Saat segizde ishteym. I work at eight.
Кечинде китеп окуйм. Kechinde kitep okuyum. In the evening I read a book.
Саат онбирде уктайм. Saat onbirde uktaym. I sleep at eleven.

Build Your Own

Swap the verbs and time words to describe your own day. Use the pattern:

Мен + [time expression] + [verb].

Мен саат алтыда турам. Кечинде иштейм. Түнде уктайм.

Practise saying your own schedule out loud before moving to the quiz.
