What Matters First

Ң ң

ng as in sing.

Ө ө

o with lips rounded forward, close to German o-umlaut.

Ү ү

u with lips rounded forward, close to German u-umlaut.

Ы ы

back vowel not found in English; memorize it as its own sound early.

Day 1 Memory Targets

  • Know that Kyrgyz in Kyrgyzstan is written in Cyrillic.
  • Spot the three Kyrgyz-specific letters: ң, ө, ү.
  • Stop misreading р as p and с as c.
  • Start noticing ы as a separate vowel, not a strange version of и.

Sources: Omniglot, Wikipedia correspondence chart. Ask follow-up questions whenever a letter still feels fuzzy.
